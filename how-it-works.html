How Dealer Sourcing Works

A simple, transparent B2B process from dealer RFQ to Japanese auction sourcing, export documents, and port-based shipment.

What Are Reference Vehicles?

The vehicles shown in our catalog are past transaction examples. They are not currently in stock. Dealers and importers can use them to compare resale prices, model demand, mileage, year range, and FOB/C&F/CIF planning before submitting a dealer RFQ for similar units from Japanese auctions.

Dealer Sourcing Step-by-Step Guide

1

Review Reference Vehicles

Use our reference catalog to compare past transaction prices, specifications, resale fit, model demand, and target FOB ranges for your market.

2

Submit Dealer RFQ

Send your buyer type, company name, monthly volume, target resale market, port, trade term, budget, and preferred model list. WhatsApp is available for faster B2B communication.

3

Confirm Quote & Pay Deposit (T/T)

After we confirm auction availability, condition, and estimated FOB/C&F/CIF amount, a deposit by Telegraphic Transfer (T/T) is required to proceed.

4

We Source from Auction

After receiving your order and deposit, we search and purchase suitable RHD vehicles from Japanese auctions on your behalf, based on the confirmed dealer requirements.

5

Photo / Sheet Confirmation & Balance

After purchase, we send photos and available condition information for confirmation. The remaining balance is requested according to the agreed order terms.

6

Export & Shipping

After receiving full payment, we arrange export and book the vessel. We will provide shipment details immediately after the booking is confirmed.

7

Documents Sent

Once the vehicle is loaded onto the vessel, we send shipping documents by email and dispatch originals by EMS or DHL: Bill of Lading, Invoice, and Export Certificate.

✓

Arrival at Destination Port

The vehicle arrives at the agreed destination port. Dealers and importers should arrange local import clearance, duty payment, registration, and local delivery.

Important Notes

⚠️ Vehicle Regulations

Each country has different import restrictions. Depending on your country, some car models may not be importable. Please check your country's vehicle registration laws before placing an order. We cannot advise on import procedures in your country.

🏦 Banking Hours

We can confirm your T/T payment only during Japanese banking hours: 9:00am – 3:00pm (JST), Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays. Please send a copy of your T/T payment form by email after remittance.

🚫 Cancellation Policy

Before auction purchase, cancellation may be possible, but any bank charges, inspection costs, inland transport costs, or other actual expenses already incurred may be deducted from the refund amount. After we have purchased the vehicle from auction on your behalf, orders cannot be cancelled. Please confirm the model, budget, resale suitability, import rules, trade term, and deposit approval before authorizing auction purchase.

🚗 Right-Hand Drive Only

All vehicles we export are Right-Hand Drive (RHD). Please confirm that RHD vehicles are legal and suitable for use in your country before placing an order.
