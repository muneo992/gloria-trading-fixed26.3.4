Dealer Shipping & Payment

Terms of trade, T/T payment flow, vessel booking, and port-based shipment details for dealer and importer orders.

Payment Terms

Accepted Payment Methods

  • Telegraphic Transfer (T/T) — only accepted method
  • Currencies accepted: USD and Japanese Yen (JPY)

Payment Process

  1. Pay a deposit by T/T to confirm the dealer order or auction sourcing instruction.
  2. After auction purchase and photo / condition confirmation, pay the remaining balance.
  3. We arrange shipment by the first available suitable vessel after receiving full payment.
  4. Please send a copy of your T/T payment form by email after each remittance for faster confirmation.

⏰ Banking Hours (Japan)

9:00am – 3:00pm (JST), Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays. T/T payments can only be confirmed during these hours.

Trade Terms

Term Includes
FOB Car cost + Port Charge + Custom Clearance + Shipping Charge
Does not include container fee or freight
C&F FOB + Freight
CIF FOB + Freight + Insurance

Reference prices in our catalog are shown as FOB prices. Dealer quotations can be prepared as FOB, C&F, or CIF when you provide the destination port and preferred trade term.

Cancellation Policy

Orders cannot be cancelled after Gloria Trading has purchased a vehicle from auction on behalf of the buyer. Before confirming an auction order, dealers and importers must carefully check the model, year, mileage, auction grade, repair history, photos, destination regulations, trade term, total estimated cost, and deposit approval.

If an order is cancelled before auction purchase, any bank charges, inspection costs, inland transport costs, or other actual expenses already incurred may be deducted from the refund amount. Refund timing depends on bank processing and international remittance conditions.

Shipping Details

🚢

Vessel Booking

We arrange shipment for dealer orders by the first available suitable vessel after receiving full payment. Shipment details are provided immediately after vessel booking is confirmed.

📄

Shipping Documents

Documents are sent by email and also dispatched as originals via EMS or DHL. See the Documents & FAQ page for the full list.

🛡️

Insurance

Marine insurance is available at an extra charge for CIF or when requested. Coverage is valid until the vehicle reaches the destination port and generally covers total loss only. Please request at the time of order.

⚓

Destination Ports

We regularly quote and ship to Tema (Ghana), Lagos (Nigeria), Cotonou (Benin), and Abidjan (Côte d’Ivoire). Other ports are available — please contact us for freight quotes.

Import Restrictions

Each destination market has different import restrictions. Dealers and importers are responsible for checking vehicle age limits, drive-side rules, inspection requirements, duty, registration rules, and local resale compliance before placing an order.

Gloria Trading has no responsibility if a vehicle is rejected at the destination port or if additional fees are incurred in your country due to import regulations.

Governing Law

Our terms of trade and contract shall be governed in all respects by the laws of Japan.
