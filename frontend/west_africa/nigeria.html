Nigeria Import Rules & Regulations

📅 Age Limit

Vehicles must be 15 years old or newer from the year of manufacture. Commercial vehicles (trucks, buses) have no age restriction.

🚗 Drive Side

Left-Hand Drive (LHD) is standard in Nigeria. RHD vehicles require conversion or special permits for registration.

💰 Duties & Taxes

Import duties and levies range from 35%–70% depending on vehicle type, engine size, and classification.

Shipping to Nigeria

🚢 Main Ports

Lagos is Nigeria's primary import hub with two major ports: Tin Can Island and Apapa. Both offer efficient container handling and customs services.

⏱️ Shipping Time

Typical transit time from Japan to Lagos is 35–45 days by sea. Port congestion may affect clearance timelines.

📋 Documentation

We provide Bill of Lading, Commercial Invoice, and Export Certificate. Customs clearance support and local agent coordination available.
