Used Cars for Sale in Ghana

Japanese right-hand drive vehicles tailored for the Ghanaian market. Reliable, affordable, and compliant with local import regulations.

Ghana Import Rules & Regulations

📅 Age Limit

Vehicles must be 10 years old or newer from the year of manufacture. Cars older than 10 years are prohibited from import.

🚗 Drive Side

Right-Hand Drive (RHD) vehicles are allowed and widely used in Ghana. All our vehicles are RHD-only.

✅ Inspection

Pre-import inspection by GSA or JAAI is required. We handle all documentation and compliance checks.

Shipping to Ghana

🚢 Main Port

Tema Port is Ghana's primary container port and the main entry point for vehicle imports. It offers modern facilities and efficient customs clearance.

⏱️ Shipping Time

Typical transit time from Japan to Tema is 40–45 days by sea. This includes port operations and standard shipping schedules.

📋 Documentation

We provide all necessary export documents: Bill of Lading, Commercial Invoice, and Export Certificate. Customs clearance support is available.
