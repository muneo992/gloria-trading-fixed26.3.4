Premium Japanese vehicles for Côte d'Ivoire's discerning buyers. We help dealers source recent-model cars that meet strict import standards and support long-term growth.

Côte d'Ivoire Import Rules & Regulations

📅 Age Limit (Strict)

Passenger cars: 5 years maximum from year of manufacture. Taxis: 5 years. Minibuses: 7 years. Trucks: 7–10 years depending on size.

🚗 Drive Side

Left-Hand Drive (LHD) is standard. RHD vehicles require conversion or special permits.

✅ Inspection

BSC (Bureau de Vérification de Conformité) inspection and Conformity Certificate required before import.

Typical transit time from Japan to Abidjan is 40–50 days by sea. Abidjan's efficiency ensures reliable delivery schedules.
