Gloria Trading — Japanese Used Car Sourcing Partner

Gloria Trading is a Japan-based vehicle export and auction sourcing company supporting overseas dealers, importers, fleet buyers, and repeat-order customers. Founded in 2006 and headquartered in Natori, Miyagi Prefecture, Japan, we specialise in right-hand drive Japanese used vehicles and port-based FOB, C&F, and CIF quotations.

Japan is widely regarded as one of the best sources of used cars globally. Japanese used cars are renowned for their low cost, excellent condition, and high specifications. We leverage our strong ties with domestic auction networks to source vehicles that precisely match our customers' requirements.

We specialise exclusively in Right-Hand Drive (RHD) vehicles, serving markets across Africa and other RHD regions. Our primary service destinations include Ghana (Tema), Nigeria (Lagos), Benin (Cotonou), and Côte d’Ivoire (Abidjan). We can also check freight and sourcing options for other right-hand drive markets upon request.

How We Build Trust

Every dealer relationship is built on transparency and dependable support at every stage.

Reference Vehicles

Past transaction examples help dealers plan budgets, models, and resale markets before placing orders.

Transparent Quotes

FOB, C&F, and CIF quotations are prepared clearly, with cost components explained before purchase confirmation.

Condition Confirmation

Photos, auction sheets, grade, mileage, and repair history are shared before authorizing auction purchase.

Clear Trade Terms

Trade terms, payment steps, shipping routes, and cancellation conditions are confirmed in advance.
