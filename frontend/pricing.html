Dealer Pricing & Trade Terms

Understanding dealer reference prices, port-based FOB/C&F/CIF quotations, and what is included in each trade term.

About Dealer Reference Prices

Prices shown in our catalog are past transaction price ranges for dealer resale planning. They are provided as a guide for importers comparing target FOB price, port cost, resale margin, and monthly sourcing plans. Actual prices depend on current auction supply, winning bid level, mileage, grade, repair history, shipping route, and your requested trade term.

All reference prices are FOB (Free On Board) Japan unless stated otherwise. For dealers, we can also prepare C&F or CIF quotations for Tema, Lagos, Cotonou, Abidjan, and other available ports.

Trade Terms Explained

Trade Term Full Name What Is Included Not Included / Notes When to Use
FOB Free On Board Vehicle cost, inland handling, Japanese port charges, Japanese export customs clearance, and loading arrangement at the Japan port. Ocean freight, marine insurance, destination port charges, import duty, registration, and local delivery are not included. Useful when a dealer or importer has their own shipping agent and wants to control freight separately.
C&F Cost & Freight Everything in FOB, plus ocean freight to the destination port such as Tema, Lagos, Cotonou, or Abidjan. Marine insurance, destination port charges, import duty, registration, and local delivery are not included. Recommended for dealers comparing landed cost to the destination port before deciding resale price.
CIF Cost, Insurance & Freight Everything in C&F, plus basic marine insurance during the sea voyage. Destination port charges, import duty, registration, inspection after arrival, and local delivery are not included. Useful when a dealer needs freight plus basic marine insurance included in the port-based quote.

Dealer Quote Request Tip

Please tell us your buyer type, company name, monthly purchase volume, target resale market, destination country, port, budget, preferred models list, trade term, and auction sheet requirement. This helps us prepare a realistic FOB, C&F, or CIF dealer quotation.
