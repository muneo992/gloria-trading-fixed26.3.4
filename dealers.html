For Dealers & Importers

Gloria Trading supports African car dealers, importers, fleet buyers, and repeat-order customers as a Japan-side sourcing partner for right-hand drive used vehicles.

Dealer Sourcing Support

We are not a live-stock marketplace. We use past transaction references and Japanese auction sourcing to help dealers purchase vehicles that fit their resale market.

1

Repeat Sourcing

Send a monthly model list, target budget, year range, mileage range, and market demand. We can search similar units repeatedly from Japanese auctions.

2

Auction Sourcing Support

We search suitable right-hand drive vehicles from Japanese auctions and confirm available photos, auction sheet details, mileage, grade, and repair history before purchase confirmation.

3

Multiple-Unit Quotes

Dealers can request one unit, several models, or repeat orders. Please share monthly purchase volume such as 1-2, 3-5, 6-10, or 10+ units.

4

Port-Based Trade Terms

FOB Japan, C&F, and CIF quotes are available for common destinations including Tema, Lagos, Cotonou, and Abidjan.

How Dealer Orders Work

1. Send Dealer RFQ

Tell us your buyer type, company name, resale country, destination port, trade term, monthly volume, budget, and preferred model list.

2. Check Auction Supply

We check current Japanese auction availability and compare it with past transaction references, budget, mileage, grade, and destination cost.

3. Confirm Quote & Condition

Before ordering, confirm the latest photos, auction sheet, grade, mileage, repair history, FOB/C&F/CIF amount, and expected shipping route.

4. Payment & Purchase

Payment is normally by T/T bank transfer. Auction purchase and cancellation conditions depend on timing, auction rules, and the confirmed order status.

5. Export Documents

We prepare export-related documents and coordinate shipment according to the agreed trade term. Import rules must be confirmed by the buyer in the destination country.

6. Repeat Orders

After the first transaction, dealers can continue sending model lists and monthly targets for faster sourcing communication.
