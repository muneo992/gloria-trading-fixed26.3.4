Used Cars for Sale in Benin

Japanese vehicles for Benin and West African re-export. Flexible import regulations and efficient logistics through Cotonou port.

Benin Import Rules & Regulations

📅 Age Limit

No specific age restriction on vehicle imports. This flexibility makes Benin an ideal entry point for regional distribution.

🚗 Drive Side

Left-Hand Drive (LHD) is standard. Cotonou serves as a major hub for regional trade and re-export.

📦 Hub Status

Cotonou is West Africa's largest free-trade zone. Vehicles can be imported, stored, and re-exported with minimal bureaucracy.

Shipping to Benin

🚢 Main Port

Cotonou Port is West Africa's largest free-trade zone and container hub. Modern facilities and efficient customs clearance make it ideal for vehicle imports and regional distribution.

⏱️ Shipping Time

Typical transit time from Japan to Cotonou is 40–45 days by sea. Cotonou's efficiency ensures quick turnaround.

📋 Documentation

We provide Bill of Lading, Commercial Invoice, and Export Certificate. Cotonou's free-trade status simplifies documentation and re-export procedures.
